Help and Support

How can we assist you?

Welcome to our Help and Support page. Here you can find answers to common questions and get help with our services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I reset my password?

A1: To reset your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

Q2: How can I contact support?

A2: You can contact support by sending an email to support@communitfx.com or by filling out the contact form on our Contact Us page.

Q3: Where can I find the user guide?

A3: The user guide is available on our website under the 'Resources' section. You can download it as a PDF or view it online.

Q4: How do I report a problem with the website?

A4: To report a problem, please use the 'Report a Problem' feature available in your profile settings or contact our support team directly.
